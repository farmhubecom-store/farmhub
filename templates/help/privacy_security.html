FarmHub is committed to protecting your personal information and maintaining your privacy. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Password (stored securely)
  • Security question and answer

Transaction Information

When you use FarmHub, we collect:

  • Order history
  • Payment information
  • Delivery addresses
  • Messages between you and sellers/buyers

Usage Information

We may collect:

  • Pages you visit
  • Products you view
  • Search queries
  • Device information

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide services: Process orders and deliveries
  • Communication: Send order updates and notifications
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Improvement: Enhance our platform and services
  • Support: Assist with inquiries and issues
We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is used only to provide and improve our services.

Information Sharing

We share information only when necessary:

With Sellers/Buyers

  • Delivery address for order fulfillment
  • Contact information for order communication
  • Order details for processing

With Service Providers

  • Payment processors (for transactions)
  • Hosting services (for platform operation)

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • To prevent fraud or security threats

Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Secure connections: HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Password protection: Your password is stored securely
  • Access controls: Limited staff access to personal data
  • Regular updates: Security patches and updates
Never share your password with anyone. FarmHub staff will never ask for your password.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

Access Your Data

  • View your account information anytime
  • See your order history
  • Review your messages

Update Your Data

  • Edit your profile information
  • Change your password
  • Update your address

Delete Your Data

  • Request account deletion
  • Note: Some data may be retained for legal/business purposes

Account Security Tips

  1. Strong password: Use a unique, complex password
  2. Keep it private: Don't share your login credentials
  3. Log out: Always log out on shared devices
  4. Update regularly: Change your password periodically
  5. Watch for phishing: We won't ask for your password via email

Cookies

FarmHub uses cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve your experience

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

FarmHub is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications
  • Platform announcements
  • Updated policy date
Review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our customer support channels.
