FarmHub charges a small commission on sales to maintain the platform and provide services. Here's everything sellers need to know about the commission system.

What Is Commission?

Commission is a percentage or fixed amount that FarmHub takes from your sales revenue. This helps cover:

  • Platform maintenance and development
  • Customer support services
  • Marketing to attract buyers
  • Payment processing
  • Server and hosting costs

How Commission Is Calculated

Commission is typically calculated in one of two ways:

Percentage-Based

  • A percentage of your total delivered sales
  • Example: 5% commission on ‚Ç±10,000 sales = ‚Ç±500 commission

Fixed Amount

  • A set amount once you reach the threshold
  • Example: ‚Ç±500 flat fee per month
The current commission rate and type are set by FarmHub administrators. Check your commission payment page for the current rates.

Commission Threshold

Commission is only charged when your monthly income reaches a certain threshold:

Below Threshold: No commission is due
At or Above Threshold: Commission becomes payable

Checking Your Commission Status

Step 1: Log in to your seller account.
Step 2: Navigate to "Commission Payment" in the sidebar.
Step 3: View your current status:
  • Monthly income so far
  • Threshold amount
  • Commission rate
  • Amount owed (if any)

How to Pay Commission

Step 1: Go to the "Commission Payment" page.
Step 2: View the amount owed and the payment QR code.
Step 3: Pay via GCash or the specified payment method by scanning the QR code.
Step 4: Take a screenshot of your payment confirmation.
Step 5: Upload the payment proof image on the commission page.
Step 6: Submit and wait for admin approval.
Step 7: Once approved, your status returns to "Clear."
Keep your payment receipts! The screenshot serves as proof of your payment if any issues arise.

Payment Approval Process

  1. You submit payment proof
  2. Admin reviews the submission
  3. If approved: Your commission is cleared
  4. If rejected: You'll be notified with the reason
Make sure your payment proof is clear and shows the correct amount. Blurry or incomplete screenshots may be rejected.

Tips for Commission Management

  • Track your earnings: Know when you're approaching the threshold
  • Pay promptly: Avoid account restrictions
  • Keep records: Save all payment confirmations
  • Budget: Set aside a portion of earnings for commission
