Ready to sell your farm products on FarmHub? Register as a seller and connect with customers looking for fresh, local produce. Here's how to get started.

Benefits of Selling on FarmHub

  • Direct sales: Sell directly to consumers without middlemen
  • Wider reach: Access customers beyond your local area
  • Easy management: Simple tools to manage products and orders
  • Build relationships: Connect with loyal customers
  • Track performance: View sales reports and analytics

Registration Requirements

Before you register, prepare the following:

  • Valid email address
  • Shop/farm name
  • Description of your farm/products
  • Contact information
  • Business address
  • Profile photo (optional but recommended)

Step-by-Step Registration

Step 1: Go to the FarmHub homepage.
Step 2: Click "Register" and select "Shop Registration" (orange button).
Step 3: Fill in your shop information:
  • Shop Name: Your farm or business name
  • Description: Describe your farm and what you sell
  • Owner Name: Your full name
Step 4: Enter your contact details:
  • Email: For account access and notifications
  • Phone: For customer inquiries
Step 5: Add your address:
  • House/Building number
  • Street
  • City/Municipality
  • District/Barangay
Step 6: Create a secure password and set up your security question.
Step 7: Click "Register Shop" to create your account.
Step 8: Log in with your email and password to access your seller dashboard!
Write a compelling shop description that highlights what makes your farm special - organic practices, family-owned history, specialty products, etc.

After Registration

Once registered, you can:

  1. Set up your shop profile: Add a profile image and complete your information
  2. Add products: List your farm products with images and pricing
  3. Start selling: Customers can now discover and order from you

Shop Profile Best Practices

Shop Name

  • Keep it memorable and easy to spell
  • Include location if relevant (e.g., "Bulacan Fresh Farm")
  • Reflect what you sell

Shop Description

  • Tell your story - how did you start farming?
  • Highlight your farming practices (organic, sustainable, etc.)
  • Mention your specialty products
  • Include your years of experience

Profile Image

  • Use a clear, professional photo
  • Consider using your farm logo
  • Show your farm or fresh products
Your shop email must be unique and not already registered as a user account or another shop.

Commission Information

FarmHub may charge a small commission on sales. This helps:

  • Maintain the platform
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve features for sellers and buyers
Learn more about commission rates and payments in the "How Commission Works" help article.
