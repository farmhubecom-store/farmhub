Ready to buy? Follow this guide to complete your purchase on FarmHub and get fresh farm products delivered to you.

Before You Start

Make sure you have:

  • Items added to your cart
  • A complete delivery address in your profile
  • Decided on your payment method

Step-by-Step Ordering Process

Step 1: Click on the cart icon to view your cart.
Step 2: Review all items in your cart. Check quantities and prices are correct.
Step 3: Select the items you want to order using the checkboxes (if applicable).
Step 4: Click "Proceed to Checkout" or "Place Order" button.
Step 5: Review your delivery address. Update it if needed.
Step 6: Select your preferred shipping/delivery option:
  • Delivery: Products delivered to your address
  • Pickup: You pick up from the seller's location
Step 7: Choose your payment method:
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Pay when you receive your order
  • GCash: Pay via GCash mobile wallet
Step 8: Review the order summary:
  • Items and quantities
  • Subtotal
  • Shipping fee (if applicable)
  • Total amount
Step 9: Click "Confirm Order" or "Place Order".
Step 10: You'll see an order confirmation with your order number. Save this for reference!
Double-check your delivery address before confirming. Once an order is placed, changing the address may not be possible.

Understanding Shipping Fees

Shipping fees depend on:

  • Your delivery location
  • The seller's location
  • The delivery option you choose
Pickup orders typically have no shipping fee. Consider this option if you're near the seller to save on delivery costs.

After Placing Your Order

  1. Order Confirmation: You'll receive a notification confirming your order
  2. Seller Processing: The seller will review and confirm your order
  3. Delivery Date: The seller will set an expected delivery date
  4. Preparation: Your order will be prepared for delivery
  5. On the Way: Order is out for delivery
  6. Delivered: You receive your fresh farm products!

Order Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:

  • Order confirmation
  • When the seller confirms your order
  • Expected delivery date
  • Delivery updates
Make sure your contact information is up-to-date so the seller can reach you about your order if needed.
