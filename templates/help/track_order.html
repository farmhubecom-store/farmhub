Stay updated on your order status from purchase to delivery. FarmHub makes it easy to track your fresh farm product orders.

Accessing Your Order History

Step 1: Log in to your FarmHub account.
Step 2: Click on your profile icon or name in the navigation bar.
Step 3: Select "Order History" from the dropdown menu.
Step 4: You'll see a list of all your past and current orders.

Viewing Order Details

Click on any order to see complete details:

  • Order ID: Unique order number for reference
  • Order Date: When you placed the order
  • Items Ordered: Products, quantities, and prices
  • Delivery Address: Where the order is being sent
  • Shipping Option: Delivery or pickup
  • Payment Method: How you're paying
  • Total Amount: Final order cost
  • Expected Delivery: When to expect your order
Save your order ID! You may need it when contacting the seller or customer support about your order.

Tracking Delivery Progress

For orders that are out for delivery:

Step 1: Go to your Order History.
Step 2: Find the order marked "On the Way" or similar.
Step 3: Click on the order to see detailed tracking information.
Step 4: You may see:
  • Seller's location
  • Your delivery address
  • Expected arrival time
  • Delivery person's contact (if available)

Getting Notifications

FarmHub will notify you when:

  • Your order is confirmed by the seller
  • The delivery date is set
  • Your order is out for delivery
  • Your order has been delivered
Check your notifications regularly by clicking the bell icon in the navigation bar.

What If There's a Problem?

  • Order delayed? Contact the seller through messages
  • Wrong items? Report immediately after receiving
  • Not delivered? Check the status and contact support
If your order shows as "Delivered" but you haven't received it, contact the seller or customer support immediately.
