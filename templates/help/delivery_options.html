FarmHub offers flexible delivery options to suit your needs. Choose between home delivery or convenient pickup from the farmer.

Available Options

üè† Home Delivery

Have your fresh farm products delivered right to your doorstep.

  • Convenience: No need to travel
  • Door-to-door: Products come to you
  • Delivery fee: May apply based on location

üìç Pickup

Collect your order directly from the seller's location.

  • No delivery fee: Usually free
  • Direct from source: Get products straight from the farm
  • Personal connection: Meet the farmer

How to Select Your Option

Step 1: Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout.
Step 2: On the checkout page, look for the "Shipping Option" or "Delivery Method" section.
Step 3: Choose your preferred option:
  • Delivery - Enter/confirm your delivery address
  • Pickup - Note the seller's pickup location
Step 4: Review any associated fees (delivery fee will be shown if applicable).
Step 5: Complete your order.

When to Choose Delivery

Home delivery is best when:

  • You're busy and can't travel to the farm
  • The seller is far from your location
  • You're ordering heavy or bulky items
  • Weather conditions make travel difficult
  • You don't have your own transportation

When to Choose Pickup

Pickup is a great choice when:

  • You want to save on delivery fees
  • You're close to the seller's location
  • You want to inspect products before taking them
  • You prefer a specific pickup time
  • You want to meet the farmer and see their operation
If you're ordering from multiple sellers, pickup might be more practical for nearby farms to save on multiple delivery fees.

Pickup Instructions

If you choose pickup:

Step 1: Note the seller's address provided in the order confirmation.
Step 2: Wait for the seller to confirm your order and provide pickup details.
Step 3: Go to the pickup location at the agreed time.
Step 4: Bring your order confirmation or ID for verification.
Step 5: Collect your products and pay (if COD was selected).

Important Considerations

Not all sellers offer both options. Some may only offer delivery or only offer pickup. Check the available options during checkout.

Delivery Considerations

  • Ensure your address is complete and accurate
  • Be available during the expected delivery time
  • Provide contact number for the delivery person

Pickup Considerations

  • Confirm pickup hours with the seller
  • Bring appropriate bags or containers if needed
  • Arrive during business hours
