Welcome to FarmHub! Creating an account is quick and easy. Follow the steps below to get started and access fresh farm products from local farmers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Go to the FarmHub homepage and click on the "Register" or "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar.
Step 2: Choose your account type:
  • User Registration - If you want to browse and buy farm products
  • Shop Registration - If you're a farmer/seller who wants to sell products
Step 3: Fill in your personal information:
  • First Name and Last Name
  • Email Address (this will be your login)
  • Password (choose a strong password)
  • Confirm your password
  • Phone Number
  • Address details (House/Building, Street, City, District)
Step 4: Set up your security question and answer. This helps you recover your password if you forget it.
Step 5: Click the "Sign Up" button to create your account.
Step 6: You'll see a success message. You can now log in with your email and password!
Use an email address you check regularly, as you'll receive order notifications and updates there.
Make sure your email is unique and not already registered in the system. Each email can only be used for one account.

What Information Do I Need?

  • Email: A valid email address for account verification and notifications
  • Password: A secure password you'll remember
  • Personal Details: Your name and contact information
  • Address: Your delivery address for orders

Account Types Explained

Buyer Account (User Registration)

Perfect for customers who want to:

  • Browse and search for fresh farm products
  • Add products to cart and place orders
  • Track order status and delivery
  • Message sellers directly
  • Leave ratings and reviews

Seller Account (Shop Registration)

Ideal for farmers and sellers who want to:

  • Create a shop and list products for sale
  • Manage inventory and stock levels
  • Process and fulfill customer orders
  • View sales reports and earnings
  • Communicate with customers
