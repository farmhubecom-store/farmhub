FarmHub makes it easy to find exactly what you're looking for. Use our search features to discover fresh farm products from local farmers.

Using the Search Bar

Step 1: Locate the search bar at the top of any page in the navigation area.
Step 2: Type the name of the product you're looking for (e.g., "tomatoes", "chicken", "mangoes").
Step 3: Press Enter or click the search icon to see results.
Step 4: Browse through the search results to find products that match your needs.
Try different search terms if you don't find what you're looking for. For example, search "eggs" instead of "chicken eggs".

Search Tips

  • Be specific: "Lakatan banana" will give better results than just "fruit"
  • Use common names: Search for products as you would normally call them
  • Try variations: Some products may be listed under different names
  • Check spelling: Make sure your search terms are spelled correctly

Browsing All Products

If you prefer to browse rather than search:

Step 1: Click on "All Products" or "Products" in the navigation menu.
Step 2: Scroll through the product grid to see available items.
Step 3: Click on any product to view more details.

Finding Products by Shop

Step 1: Click on "Shops" in the navigation menu.
Step 2: Browse through available farmer shops.
Step 3: Click on a shop to view all their products.
Visiting individual shops is great when you want to order multiple items from the same farmer for combined delivery.

Understanding Search Results

Each product in search results shows:

  • Product Image: A photo of the item
  • Product Name: The name of the product
  • Price: Cost per unit (kg, piece, etc.)
  • Seller/Shop: Which farmer is selling it
  • Rating: Customer reviews (if available)
