Wondering when your fresh farm products will arrive? Here's what you need to know about delivery timing on FarmHub.

How Delivery Times Work

Unlike traditional e-commerce, FarmHub connects you directly with farmers. Delivery times are set by individual sellers based on:

  • Harvest schedules
  • Product availability
  • Delivery routes and schedules
  • Distance to your location
  • Order volume

Typical Delivery Timeline

Order Confirmation: Usually within 24 hours of placing your order
Delivery Date Set: The seller sets an expected delivery date when confirming your order
Typical Delivery: Most orders are delivered within 1-3 days after confirmation

Same-Day Delivery

Some sellers may offer same-day delivery for:

  • Orders placed early in the morning
  • Customers in nearby areas
  • Products that are already harvested and ready
Same-day delivery is not guaranteed. Check with the seller or order early to improve your chances.

Checking Estimated Delivery

To see your expected delivery date:

Step 1: Go to your Order History.
Step 2: Find your order and check the status.
Step 3: The delivery date appears once the seller confirms your order.

Delivery Time for Pickup Orders

If you chose pickup:

  • The seller will notify you when your order is ready
  • Typically ready within 1-2 days
  • Pickup hours depend on the seller's schedule
  • Coordinate with the seller for a convenient pickup time

Delays and Issues

What Causes Delays?

  • Unexpected weather conditions
  • High order volumes during peak seasons
  • Product availability issues
  • Transportation problems
  • Address or contact issues

What To Do If Delayed

  1. Check your order status for updates
  2. Wait for notification from the seller
  3. Contact the seller through messages if no update
  4. Be patient - fresh products are worth the wait!
If your order is significantly delayed with no communication, contact customer support for assistance.

Planning Your Orders

Tips for getting products when you need them:

  • Order ahead: Don't wait until the last minute
  • Communicate: Let sellers know if you have time constraints
  • Be flexible: Have backup plans for time-sensitive needs
  • Regular orders: Build relationships with reliable sellers
