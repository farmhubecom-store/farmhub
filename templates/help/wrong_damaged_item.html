Discovered something wrong with your order? Don't worry - here's exactly what to do to resolve the issue quickly and get the resolution you deserve.

‚ö° Act Immediately

Time is critical with farm products! Report issues as soon as you discover them - ideally within hours, definitely within 24 hours of delivery.

Step 1: Document the Issue

Before doing anything else, take photos:

For Wrong Items:

  • Photo of what you received
  • Photo showing packaging/labels
  • Screenshot of what you ordered

For Damaged Items:

  • Photos of the damage (multiple angles)
  • Photo of the packaging condition
  • Photo showing extent of damage
  • Close-up photos of affected areas

For Spoiled/Rotten Products:

  • Photos showing the spoilage
  • Photos of discoloration, mold, or decay
  • Compare with any good items if partial spoilage
Take photos BEFORE disposing of or altering any items. These serve as your evidence.

Step 2: Contact the Seller

Step 1: Log in to your FarmHub account.
Step 2: Go to Messages and find the seller's conversation (or start a new one).
Step 3: Send a clear message including:
  • Your Order ID
  • What the problem is
  • Photos of the issue
  • What you'd like as resolution

Sample Message for Wrong Item:

"Hi, I received my order #123 today. Unfortunately, I ordered Lakatan Bananas but received Saba Bananas instead. I've attached photos showing what I received. Could you please send the correct product or arrange a refund? Thank you."

Sample Message for Damaged Item:

"Hello, my order #456 arrived today but the tomatoes were badly bruised and some are already spoiled. I've attached photos of the damage. Please advise on how we can resolve this. Thank you."

Step 3: Wait for Seller Response

Give the seller reasonable time to respond:

  • Most sellers respond within 24 hours
  • Be patient but persistent
  • If no response in 48 hours, send a follow-up

Step 4: Discuss Resolution

Work with the seller on a fair resolution:

Common Resolutions:

üîÑ Replacement: Seller sends the correct or fresh product
üí∞ Full Refund: You get all your money back
üíµ Partial Refund: You keep the item at a reduced price

What NOT to Do

  • ‚ùå Don't dispose of items before documenting
  • ‚ùå Don't wait too long to report
  • ‚ùå Don't be rude or threatening to the seller
  • ‚ùå Don't exaggerate the issue
  • ‚ùå Don't demand unreasonable compensation

If Seller Is Unresponsive

If the seller doesn't respond or refuses to help:

Step 1: Wait at least 48 hours after your initial message.
Step 2: Send a polite follow-up message.
Step 3: If still no response, contact FarmHub customer support.
Step 4: Provide all documentation: order details, photos, message history.

Preventing Future Issues

  • Check reviews: Buy from highly-rated sellers
  • Inspect on delivery: Check products before the delivery person leaves
  • Read descriptions: Make sure you understand what you're ordering
  • Communicate: Ask questions before ordering if unsure
Most sellers on FarmHub are honest farmers who want happy customers. Issues are usually resolved amicably when handled properly.
