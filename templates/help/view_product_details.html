Before making a purchase, it's important to review all the details about a product. Here's how to view complete product information on FarmHub.

Accessing Product Details

Step 1: Browse products through search, category filter, or by visiting a shop.
Step 2: Click on any product card or product image to open the detailed view.
Step 3: The product detail page will display all available information about the item.

What Information Is Available?

Basic Product Information

  • Product Name: The name of the farm product
  • Product Image: Clear photo(s) of the item
  • Price: Cost per unit (e.g., â‚±50.00/kg)
  • Unit Type: How the product is sold (per kilogram, per piece, per bundle, etc.)
  • Category: Product type (Fruits, Vegetables, Poultry, etc.)

Availability Information

  • Available Stock: How many units are currently available
  • Sold Count: How many units have been sold
  • Status: Whether the product is in stock or out of stock

Seller Information

  • Shop Name: The farmer/seller's shop name
  • Shop Rating: Average customer rating for the shop
  • Shop Location: Where the farm/seller is located

Customer Reviews

  • Product Rating: Average star rating from customers
  • Number of Reviews: How many customers have rated the product
  • Review Comments: What customers say about the product
  • Review Images: Photos uploaded by customers with their reviews

Understanding Product Units

Products on FarmHub are sold in different units:

  • Per Kilogram (kg): Price is for 1 kilogram of the product
  • Per Piece (pc): Price is for one individual item
  • Per Bundle: Price is for a bundled set of items
  • Per Pack: Price is for a packaged amount
  • Per Tray: Common for eggs and similar products
Always check the unit type before ordering. A low price might be per gram instead of per kilogram!

Reading Customer Reviews

Customer reviews help you make informed decisions:

Step 1: Scroll down on the product page to find the reviews section.
Step 2: Look at the overall star rating (1-5 stars).
Step 3: Read individual comments from customers who bought the product.
Step 4: Check review images if available to see what customers received.
Reviews are from verified buyers who have actually purchased and received the product.

Contacting the Seller

If you have questions about a product:

Step 1: Click on the shop name to visit the seller's page.
Step 2: Use the "Message" feature to contact the seller directly.
Step 3: Ask your questions and wait for the seller to respond.
