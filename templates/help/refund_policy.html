Understanding FarmHub's refund policy helps you know your rights and what to expect when issues arise. Here's our complete refund policy for farm product purchases.

Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund if:

‚úÖ Eligible for Refund

  • Non-delivery: Order was never delivered
  • Wrong product: You received something different from what you ordered
  • Damaged products: Items arrived damaged, spoiled, or unsafe to consume
  • Significant quality issues: Products substantially differ from description
  • Short quantity: You received less than the quantity you paid for
  • Order cancelled by seller: Seller cancelled after payment

‚ùå Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of mind: You no longer want the product
  • Buyer-caused damage: Damage occurred after delivery due to your handling
  • Improper storage: Products spoiled due to your storage conditions
  • Minor variations: Small natural variations in farm products
  • Late reporting: Issues reported too long after delivery
  • No evidence: Claims without photographic proof
Farm products naturally vary in appearance. Minor differences in size, color, or shape don't qualify for refunds.

Refund Timeline

Stage Timeline
Report issue Within 24 hours of delivery (recommended)
Seller response 1-2 business days
Resolution decision 1-3 business days
Refund processing 3-7 business days (depends on payment method)

How Refunds Are Processed

For Cash on Delivery (COD)

If you paid with COD and are eligible for a refund:

  • Seller may arrange a bank transfer or GCash refund
  • You may receive store credit
  • Seller may deliver replacement products

For Digital Payments (GCash)

If you paid via GCash:

  • Refund to your GCash account
  • Processing time: 3-7 business days

Refund Process

Step 1: Report the issue to the seller with photos and details.
Step 2: Seller reviews your claim.
Step 3: If approved, seller initiates the refund.
Step 4: Refund is processed to your original payment method.
Step 5: You receive confirmation when refund is complete.

Partial vs Full Refunds

Full Refund

You receive your entire payment back when:

  • Order was never delivered
  • Entire order was wrong or damaged
  • Products are completely unusable

Partial Refund

You receive part of your payment when:

  • Only some items in the order had issues
  • Products are partially usable
  • Quality was lower than expected but still acceptable
Partial refunds are often a fair compromise when the issue isn't severe enough for a full refund but still warrants compensation.

Dispute Resolution

If you and the seller can't agree:

  1. Try to negotiate: Work with the seller on a fair solution
  2. Contact FarmHub Support: We can mediate disputes
  3. Provide evidence: Photos, messages, and order details help
  4. Accept the decision: FarmHub's decision is final
False or fraudulent refund claims are taken seriously. Accounts found making dishonest claims may be suspended.

Best Practices

  • Inspect immediately: Check products right when they arrive
  • Document everything: Take photos before opening packaging
  • Report promptly: Don't wait to report issues
  • Be honest: Only request refunds for legitimate issues
  • Communicate clearly: Explain the problem with specifics
