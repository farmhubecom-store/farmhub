FarmHub connects you directly with local farmers who handle the delivery of your fresh farm products. Here's everything you need to know about how delivery works.

The Delivery Process

Step 1 - Order Placed: You place an order for farm products.
Step 2 - Seller Confirmation: The farmer/seller confirms your order and sets a delivery date.
Step 3 - Preparation: The seller prepares and packages your fresh products.
Step 4 - Dispatch: Your order is dispatched for delivery.
Step 5 - Delivery: The products are delivered to your specified address.
Step 6 - Confirmation: Order is marked as delivered once you receive it.

Delivery Options

Home Delivery

Products are brought directly to your doorstep.

  • Convenient for busy customers
  • Delivery fee may apply based on location
  • Products delivered fresh from the farm

Pickup

You pick up your order from the seller's location.

  • Often free of charge
  • Great if you're near the farm
  • Meet the farmer who grew your food
If delivery fees are high, consider the pickup option to save money, especially for large orders.

Delivery Areas

Delivery availability depends on:

  • Seller location: Each farmer has their own delivery range
  • Your address: Must be within the seller's delivery area
  • Distance: Longer distances may have higher fees or limited availability
Check the seller's shop page or contact them directly to confirm they deliver to your area.

Delivery Fees

Delivery fees are determined by:

  • Distance from the seller to your address
  • The seller's delivery pricing policy
  • Order size and weight

Common fee structures:

  • Flat rate: Same fee regardless of order size
  • Distance-based: Fee increases with distance
  • Free delivery: Some sellers offer free delivery above a minimum order

Freshness Guarantee

FarmHub farmers take pride in delivering fresh products:

  • Products are harvested close to delivery time
  • Proper packaging maintains freshness
  • Direct farm-to-table reduces handling time
Inspect your products upon delivery. Report any quality issues immediately to the seller.

What to Expect on Delivery Day

  1. Be available at your delivery address during the expected time
  2. Have payment ready if you chose Cash on Delivery
  3. Inspect products before accepting delivery
  4. Sign or confirm receipt if required

Delivery Issues

What if I'm not home?

  • Leave instructions for where to leave the package
  • Ask someone to receive the delivery on your behalf
  • Contact the seller to reschedule

What if delivery is late?

  • Check your order status in Order History
  • Contact the seller through messages
  • Weather or traffic may cause delays
