Track your business performance on FarmHub. Learn how to view your sales data, earnings reports, and understand your income from selling farm products.

Accessing Your Dashboard

Step 1: Log in to your seller account.
Step 2: You'll be taken to your Seller Dashboard.
Step 3: View your income and sales statistics at a glance.

Dashboard Income Overview

These figures only include completed (delivered) orders. Pending or cancelled orders are not counted.

Recent Sales

The dashboard shows your most recent sales:

  • Product Name: What was sold
  • Quantity: How many units
  • Price: Sale price per unit
  • Subtotal: Total for that item
  • Date Sold: When the order was delivered

Order History

For detailed sales information:

Step 1: Click "Order History" in the sidebar.
Step 2: View all orders received by your shop.
Step 3: See details including:
  • Order ID and date
  • Buyer information
  • Items ordered with quantities
  • Order total and shipping
  • Payment method
  • Order status

Processing Orders

To increase your earnings, process orders efficiently:

  1. Confirm orders promptly: Review and confirm new orders quickly
  2. Set realistic delivery dates: Customers appreciate accurate timing
  3. Deliver on time: Meet the promised delivery date
  4. Mark as delivered: Update status when orders are completed

Earnings Tips

Increase Sales

  • Keep products in stock
  • Use attractive product photos
  • Offer competitive prices
  • Respond to customer messages quickly
  • Provide excellent service for good reviews

Track Performance

  • Check dashboard daily
  • Note which products sell best
  • Identify slow-moving items
  • Adjust pricing and inventory accordingly
Remember that commission may be deducted from your earnings once you reach certain thresholds. Check the commission section for details.
