Find quick answers to the most common questions about using FarmHub.

Account & Registration

Q: How do I create an account?

Click "Register" on the homepage, choose "User Registration" for buying or "Shop Registration" for selling, fill in your details, and submit. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, answer your security question, and create a new password. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: Can I have both a buyer and seller account?

Yes, but they must use different email addresses. You can register as a buyer with one email and as a seller with another.

Q: How do I update my profile information?

Log in, click your profile icon, select "Edit Account", make changes, and save. Learn more ‚Üí

Ordering & Payment

Q: How do I place an order?

Add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, confirm your delivery address, choose shipping and payment options, then confirm your order. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

FarmHub currently accepts Cash on Delivery (COD) and GCash payments.

Q: Can I cancel my order?

Contact the seller immediately through messages. If the order hasn't been confirmed yet, they may be able to cancel it. Once confirmed or dispatched, cancellation may not be possible.

Q: How do I track my order?

Go to Order History from your profile menu. Click on your order to see its current status and tracking information. Learn more ‚Üí

Delivery

Q: How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by seller and location. Most orders are delivered within 1-3 days after confirmation. The seller sets a specific delivery date when confirming your order. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: Is there a delivery fee?

Delivery fees depend on the seller and your location. The fee is shown at checkout before you confirm your order. Pickup is usually free.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

Contact the seller immediately. If the order hasn't been dispatched, they may be able to update the address. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: What if I'm not home during delivery?

Leave instructions in your order notes, have someone receive it on your behalf, or coordinate with the seller/delivery person.

Products & Quality

Q: Are the products fresh?

FarmHub connects you directly with farmers who harvest and deliver fresh products. Check seller ratings and reviews for quality feedback. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: Can I return products?

Yes, if you received wrong, damaged, or poor-quality items. Report issues immediately with photos and contact the seller. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: What if I receive a damaged item?

Take photos immediately and contact the seller through messages. Most sellers will offer a replacement or refund. Learn more ‚Üí

For Sellers

Q: How do I start selling on FarmHub?

Register a shop account, complete your profile, add products with photos and pricing, and wait for approval. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: How do I list a product?

From your seller dashboard, go to Products ‚Üí Add Product. Fill in details, upload photos, set pricing and stock, then submit. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: How does commission work?

FarmHub charges a small commission on sales once your monthly income exceeds a threshold. Pay via GCash and upload proof for approval. Learn more ‚Üí

Q: How do I get paid?

You receive payment directly from customers (COD or GCash). FarmHub does not hold your payments.

Q: How do I view my sales?

Your seller dashboard shows daily, monthly, and yearly income. Order History shows all transactions. Learn more ‚Üí

Communication

Q: How do I message a seller?

Visit the seller's shop page or your Order History and use the Messages feature. You can ask questions before ordering or discuss order issues.

Q: How do I leave a review?

After your order is delivered, go to Order History, find the order, and click on the product to leave a rating and review.

Privacy & Security

Q: Is my personal information safe?

Yes. We use secure connections and don't sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy ‚Üí

Q: Who can see my information?

Sellers can see your delivery address and contact info for order fulfillment. Your password and financial details are never shared.
