FarmHub is committed to connecting you with quality farm products. Here's how we work to ensure you receive fresh, safe, and high-quality products.

Our Quality Approach

FarmHub employs multiple measures to maintain product quality:

  • Seller verification: Farmers register with their details
  • Product approval: Listings are reviewed before going live
  • Customer reviews: Real feedback from actual buyers
  • Rating system: Stars help identify top sellers
  • Direct communication: Message sellers with questions

Product Approval Process

Before products appear on FarmHub:

Step 1: Seller submits a product listing with details and photos.
Step 2: FarmHub admin reviews the listing.
Step 3: Listing is checked for:
  • Accurate description
  • Clear product photos
  • Appropriate pricing
  • Valid category
Step 4: If approved, product becomes visible to buyers.

Seller Accountability

Sellers are held to quality standards through:

Ratings and Reviews

  • Buyers rate products after delivery
  • Ratings are visible to all users
  • Low ratings affect seller visibility

Customer Feedback

  • Written reviews provide detailed feedback
  • Photo reviews show real product quality
  • Helps other buyers make decisions

Seller Reputation

  • Overall shop rating displayed
  • History of customer satisfaction
  • Encourages sellers to maintain quality
Always check seller ratings and reviews before ordering, especially from a new seller. It's your best indicator of product quality.

What We Expect from Sellers

FarmHub sellers are expected to:

  • Provide accurate product descriptions
  • Use real photos of their actual products
  • Maintain freshness until delivery
  • Package products appropriately
  • Deliver within promised timeframes
  • Respond to customer inquiries promptly
  • Handle issues fairly and professionally

Understanding Farm Product Quality

Natural farm products may vary in:

  • Size: Natural variation in product sizes
  • Color: Seasonal and natural color differences
  • Shape: Not all produce is perfectly uniform
  • Appearance: Small blemishes don't affect quality
These natural variations are normal for farm-fresh products and don't indicate quality problems. In fact, they often indicate the product is genuinely fresh and local!

How to Identify Quality Products

Before Ordering:

  • Read product descriptions carefully
  • Look at product photos closely
  • Check seller ratings and reviews
  • Ask questions if unsure

Upon Delivery:

  • Inspect products immediately
  • Check for freshness indicators
  • Compare to what was advertised
  • Report issues right away

Reporting Quality Issues

If you receive poor quality products:

  1. Document with photos immediately
  2. Contact the seller through messages
  3. Explain the quality issue clearly
  4. Request appropriate resolution
  5. Leave an honest review to help other buyers
Repeated quality complaints against a seller may result in their account being reviewed or suspended to protect buyers.

Your Role in Quality

You can help maintain marketplace quality by:

  • Leaving honest reviews: Help other buyers and sellers improve
  • Reporting issues: Let us know about consistent problems
  • Communicating clearly: Specific feedback helps sellers improve
  • Being fair: Distinguish between real issues and minor variations
