Before placing an order, it's important to check if products are available. Here's how to verify stock availability on FarmHub.

Checking Stock on Product Pages

Step 1: Navigate to the product you're interested in by clicking on it.
Step 2: Look for the "Available" or "In Stock" information on the product page.
Step 3: The number shown indicates how many units are currently available for purchase.

Understanding Availability Indicators

Available/In Stock

When a product shows "Available: X" or "In Stock":

  • The product can be ordered
  • The number indicates the maximum quantity you can order
  • Stock is reserved on a first-come, first-served basis

Out of Stock

When a product shows "Out of Stock" or "Available: 0":

  • The product cannot be ordered at this time
  • You cannot add it to your cart
  • Check back later as stock may be replenished

Low Stock

When stock is limited:

  • A warning may indicate "Only X left"
  • Order soon to avoid missing out
  • Consider ordering what you need before it runs out
Fresh farm products may have limited availability depending on harvests and seasons. Popular items can sell out quickly!

What Affects Product Availability?

  • Seasonal Products: Some fruits and vegetables are only available during certain seasons
  • Harvest Cycles: Availability depends on when farmers harvest their crops
  • Demand: Popular products may sell out faster
  • Weather: Weather conditions can affect crop yields
  • Multiple Orders: Other customers may be ordering the same products

Tips for Getting Products You Want

  1. Order early: Don't wait too long if you see something you want
  2. Check regularly: Stock is replenished as farmers harvest more
  3. Follow shops: Get to know which shops have your favorite products
  4. Message sellers: Ask about expected restocking dates
  5. Be flexible: Consider alternatives if your preferred product is unavailable
Stock numbers update in real-time, but there may be a brief delay. If you're ordering a popular item, complete your purchase quickly to secure your order.

What If a Product Becomes Unavailable?

If a product in your cart becomes unavailable before you checkout:

  • You'll be notified when trying to place the order
  • You may need to adjust quantities or remove the item
  • Check if the seller has similar alternatives
Adding items to your cart doesn't reserve them. Complete your checkout to secure your order.
