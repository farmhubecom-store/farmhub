Forgot your password? Don't worry! FarmHub makes it easy to recover access to your account using your security question.

Step-by-Step Password Reset

Step 1: Go to the FarmHub login page.
Step 2: Click on the "Forgot Password?" link below the login form.
Step 3: Enter your registered email address and click "Continue" or "Submit".
Step 4: Your security question will appear. Answer it correctly (the same answer you provided during registration).
Step 5: If your answer is correct, you'll be able to create a new password.
Step 6: Enter your new password and confirm it by typing it again.
Step 7: Click "Reset Password" or "Save".
Step 8: You can now log in with your new password!
Choose a strong password that's easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess. Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

What If I Forgot My Security Answer?

If you can't remember your security question answer, please contact customer support for assistance. You may need to verify your identity through alternative means.

For security, there's no way to view your original security answer. Make sure to remember both your password and security answer.

Tips for Creating a Strong Password

  • Use at least 8 characters
  • Include uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Add numbers and special characters (!@#$%)
  • Avoid using personal information like birthdays
  • Don't use the same password as other websites
  • Consider using a passphrase (e.g., "FreshFarm2024!")

Account Security Best Practices

  1. Don't share your password - Keep it private
  2. Log out on shared devices - Always log out when using public computers
  3. Update regularly - Change your password every few months
  4. Be cautious of phishing - FarmHub will never ask for your password via email
If you suspect someone has accessed your account without permission, reset your password immediately and contact support.
