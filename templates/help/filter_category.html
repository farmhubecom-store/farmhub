How to Filter by Category

Step 1: Go to the "All Products" page or shop page.
Step 2: Look for the category filter buttons or dropdown menu, usually at the top of the product list.
Step 3: Click on the category you want to view (e.g., "Fruits", "Vegetables", "Poultry").
Step 4: The product list will update to show only products in that category.
Step 5: To see all products again, click "All" or clear the filter.

Filtering Within a Shop

When viewing a specific farmer's shop:

Step 1: Visit the shop page by clicking on a shop name.
Step 2: Use the category filter to see only specific types of products that shop offers.
Step 3: This helps when a shop has many products and you're looking for something specific.
If you're looking for a specific product, combine category filtering with the search function for best results.

Tips for Finding Products

  • Start broad, then narrow: First browse a category, then look for specific items
  • Check multiple categories: Some products might appear in different categories
  • Look at "All" first: If you're not sure which category, browse all products
  • Check shop specialties: Some farms specialize in certain categories
Product categories are set by the farmers when they list their items. If you can't find a product, try using the search bar or contact the seller directly.
