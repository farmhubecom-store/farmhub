Need help? FarmHub's customer support team is here to assist you. Here's how to reach us and what to expect.

Before Contacting Support

You might find quick answers by:

  • Browsing the Help Center: Check our help articles for common questions
  • Reading the FAQ: Many common questions are answered there
  • Contacting the seller: For order-specific issues, message the seller first

How to Get Support

Option 1: Message the Seller (For Order Issues)

For issues with specific orders:

Step 1: Go to your Order History.
Step 2: Find the relevant order.
Step 3: Use the Messages feature to contact the seller directly.
Step 4: Most issues can be resolved directly with the seller.
Sellers are usually the fastest way to resolve order-specific issues like wrong items, delays, or quality concerns.

Option 2: Help Center

Our Help Center (where you are now!) provides:

  • Step-by-step guides
  • Answers to common questions
  • Policy information
  • Troubleshooting tips

Option 3: In-App Notifications

Check your notifications for:

  • System announcements
  • Updates about your account
  • Important notices

What to Include When Contacting Support

Help us help you faster by including:

  • Your account email: So we can find your account
  • Order ID: If the issue is about an order
  • Detailed description: What happened and when
  • Screenshots: Visual evidence helps
  • What you've tried: Steps you've already taken
  • Desired outcome: What resolution would help

Types of Issues We Can Help With

Account Issues

  • Login problems
  • Password recovery issues
  • Account security concerns
  • Profile update problems

Order Issues (After Seller Contact)

  • Disputes with sellers
  • Unresponsive sellers
  • Refund complications
  • Delivery problems

Platform Issues

  • Technical errors
  • Payment processing problems
  • Bug reports
  • Feature questions

Policy Questions

  • Refund policies
  • Commission information
  • Terms of service
  • Privacy concerns

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within:

Issue Type Response Time
Urgent (account security) Within 24 hours
Order disputes 1-2 business days
General inquiries 2-3 business days
Feature requests 3-5 business days
Response times may vary during holidays or peak periods. Thank you for your patience!

Tips for Faster Resolution

  1. Be specific: Clearly describe your issue
  2. Provide details: Include order IDs, dates, and names
  3. Share evidence: Screenshots and photos help
  4. Check spam: Look for our response in your spam folder
  5. Follow up politely: If you haven't heard back after the expected time

What Support Cannot Help With

  • Price negotiations with sellers
  • Changing other users' behavior
  • Issues outside FarmHub's platform
  • Requests violating our terms of service
Please be respectful when contacting support. Abusive or threatening communication will not be tolerated.
