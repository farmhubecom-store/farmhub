Received something that isn't quite right? FarmHub wants you to be satisfied with your purchases. Here's how to request a return or report an issue.

When Can You Request a Return?

You may be eligible for a return if:

  • Wrong product delivered: You received something different from what you ordered
  • Damaged items: Products arrived damaged or spoiled
  • Quality issues: Products don't meet the described quality
  • Missing items: Part of your order is missing
  • Incorrect quantity: You received less than you ordered

How to Report an Issue

Step 1: Act quickly! Report issues as soon as you receive your order, ideally within 24 hours.
Step 2: Take photos of the issue:
  • Photos of damaged/wrong products
  • Photos of packaging if relevant
  • Photo of your order receipt/details
Step 3: Go to your Order History.
Step 4: Find the affected order and click to view details.
Step 5: Contact the seller through the Messages feature.
Step 6: Explain the issue clearly:
  • What was wrong?
  • What did you expect?
  • What resolution would you like?
Step 7: Share your photos as evidence.
Step 8: Wait for the seller's response and work together on a resolution.
Be polite and specific when reporting issues. Farmers on FarmHub are generally understanding and want to make things right.

What to Include in Your Report

  • Order ID: Your unique order number
  • Product name: Which item has the issue
  • Description: What's wrong with the product
  • Photos: Visual evidence of the problem
  • Desired outcome: What would resolve the issue for you

Possible Resolutions

Depending on the situation, resolutions may include:

Replacement

  • Seller sends the correct/fresh product
  • Usually at no additional cost
  • May require returning the wrong item

Partial Refund

  • For quality issues that are minor
  • You keep the product at a reduced price

Full Refund

  • For serious issues or non-delivery
  • Product may need to be returned

Store Credit

  • Credit for future purchases
  • Good for maintaining relationship with seller
Fresh farm products are perishable. Report issues immediately! Delayed reports may be harder to verify and resolve.

Returns for Farm Products

Because farm products are perishable, returns work differently than regular retail:

  • No "change of mind" returns: You can't return because you simply don't want it anymore
  • Freshness matters: Products that were fine on delivery but spoiled due to your storage aren't eligible
  • Documentation is key: Photos taken at delivery time are most convincing
  • Physical returns may not be required: For perishable items, sellers may not want them back

Tips for a Smooth Resolution

  1. Inspect on delivery: Check products when you receive them
  2. Document immediately: Take photos right away if there's an issue
  3. Contact seller first: Most issues can be resolved directly
  4. Be reasonable: Work with the seller on a fair solution
  5. Keep communication on platform: Messages serve as records
If you cannot resolve the issue with the seller, you can contact FarmHub customer support for assistance.
