Access your FarmHub account securely by following these simple steps for logging in and out.

How to Log In

Step 1: Go to the FarmHub homepage and click on the "Login" button in the navigation bar.
Step 2: Enter your registered email address in the Email field.
Step 3: Enter your password in the Password field.
Step 4: Click the "Login" button to access your account.
If you have both a buyer and seller account, make sure you're logging into the correct one. The system will direct you to the appropriate dashboard.

How to Log Out

Step 1: Look for your profile icon or name in the top right corner of the navigation bar.
Step 2: Click on it to reveal the dropdown menu.
Step 3: Select "Logout" from the menu.
Step 4: You'll be logged out and redirected to the homepage.
Always log out when using a shared or public computer to protect your account and personal information.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Forgot Your Password?

If you can't remember your password:

  1. Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Answer your security question
  4. Create a new password

Account Not Found?

If you see an "account not found" error:

  • Double-check your email address for typos
  • Make sure you're using the email you registered with
  • If you're a seller, ensure you're logging into the shop login (not user login)

Incorrect Password?

If your password isn't working:

  • Check if Caps Lock is on
  • Make sure there are no extra spaces
  • Try using the "Forgot Password" feature to reset it
For security, your account may be temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts. Wait a few minutes before trying again.

Staying Logged In

Your session will remain active while you browse FarmHub. However, for security purposes, you may be automatically logged out after a period of inactivity.
