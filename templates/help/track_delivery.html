Know exactly where your order is and when to expect it. FarmHub provides order tracking to keep you informed every step of the way.

Accessing Order Tracking

Step 1: Log in to your FarmHub account.
Step 2: Go to "Order History" from your profile menu.
Step 3: Find the order you want to track.
Step 4: Click on the order or the "Track" button to see detailed tracking information.

Tracking Information Available

When tracking your order, you can see:

  • Order Status: Current stage of your order
  • Order Date: When you placed the order
  • Confirmation Date: When the seller confirmed
  • Expected Delivery: Estimated arrival date
  • Seller Location: Where your order is coming from
  • Your Delivery Address: Where it's being delivered
  • Seller Contact: How to reach the seller
If your order shows "Out for Delivery," stay available at your delivery address. Keep your phone nearby in case the delivery person needs to contact you.

Delivery Day Tracking

On the day of delivery:

  1. Check your order status in the morning
  2. Ensure you're available at the delivery address
  3. Keep your phone charged and with you
  4. Watch for notifications from FarmHub
  5. Be ready to receive and inspect your products

What If Tracking Shows Issues?

Status Not Updating?

  • Refresh the page
  • Check back in a few hours
  • Contact the seller if it's been over 24 hours with no update

Delivery Date Changed?

  • Sellers may update delivery dates based on availability
  • You'll be notified of any changes
  • Contact the seller if you have concerns

Marked Delivered But Not Received?

  • Check with family members or neighbors
  • Contact the seller immediately
  • Report to customer support if unresolved
If there's a discrepancy between the tracking status and what you've actually received, contact the seller right away.
