Keep your FarmHub profile up-to-date to ensure smooth ordering and delivery. Here's how to update your personal information.

For Buyers (User Accounts)

Step 1: Log in to your FarmHub account.
Step 2: Click on your profile icon or name in the navigation bar.
Step 3: Select "Edit Account" or "My Account" from the dropdown menu.
Step 4: Update any of the following information:
  • First Name and Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Address (House/Building, Street, City, District)
  • Profile Picture
Step 5: Click "Save Changes" or "Update" to apply your changes.

For Sellers (Shop Accounts)

Step 1: Log in to your shop account.
Step 2: Navigate to the seller dashboard using the sidebar menu.
Step 3: Click on "Account" or "Edit Shop" in the sidebar.
Step 4: Update your shop information:
  • Shop Name
  • Shop Description
  • Owner Name
  • Contact Email
  • Phone Number
  • Shop Address
  • Profile Image
Step 5: Click "Save" to update your shop profile.

Uploading a Profile Picture

Step 1: In your account settings, find the profile image section.
Step 2: Click "Choose File" or "Upload".
Step 3: Select an image from your device. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF.
Step 4: Save your changes to apply the new profile picture.
Use a clear, professional photo for your profile. For shops, consider using your logo or a picture of your farm/products.

Changing Your Password

  1. Go to your account settings
  2. Find the password section
  3. Enter your new password
  4. Confirm the new password by typing it again
  5. Save your changes
When changing your email, make sure the new email is not already registered. You'll need to use the new email for future logins.

Why Keep Your Profile Updated?

  • Accurate Deliveries: Your address is used for order deliveries
  • Communication: Sellers may need to contact you about your orders
  • Account Recovery: Your email is needed to reset your password
  • Trust: Complete profiles help build trust in the marketplace
