Keep your delivery address current to ensure your farm products reach you without any issues. Here's how to update your address.

Updating Your Default Address

Step 1: Log in to your FarmHub account.
Step 2: Click on your profile icon or name in the navigation bar.
Step 3: Select "Edit Account" from the dropdown menu.
Step 4: Find the address section and update the following fields:
  • House/Building Number: Your house number or building name
  • Street: Street name and any additional location details
  • City/Municipality: Your city or municipality
  • District/Barangay: Your district or barangay
Step 5: Click "Save Changes" or "Update" to save your new address.
Be as specific as possible with your address. Include landmarks or special instructions to help delivery persons find you easily.

Changing Address During Checkout

You can also modify your delivery address when placing an order:

Step 1: Proceed to checkout with items in your cart.
Step 2: Review the delivery address shown on the checkout page.
Step 3: If you need to change it, look for an "Edit" or "Change Address" option.
Step 4: Enter the new address details.
Step 5: Continue with your order.

Address Tips

Include These Details:

  • Complete house/unit/building number
  • Street name
  • Subdivision or village name (if applicable)
  • Barangay
  • City/Municipality
  • Province
  • Landmarks (e.g., "near the market", "beside the church")

Delivery Instructions:

Consider adding helpful notes like:

  • "Leave with security guard if not home"
  • "Gate code: 1234"
  • "Call upon arrival"
  • "Look for the blue gate"
Make sure your address is accurate before placing an order. Once an order is confirmed, changing the delivery address may not be possible or may cause delays.

Can I Change Address After Ordering?

Changing your address after placing an order depends on the order status:

Order is still "Pending"

  • Contact the seller immediately through messages
  • Request an address change before they confirm
  • The seller may be able to accommodate your request

Order is already "Confirmed" or "On the Way"

  • Contact the seller or delivery person immediately
  • Changes may not be possible at this stage
  • Additional fees may apply for rerouting
To avoid delivery issues, always double-check your address before confirming your order.

Common Address Problems

Address Not Found?

  • Check for typos in your address
  • Add more landmarks or details
  • Include your phone number for the driver to call

Delivery to Wrong Location?

  • Update your address immediately
  • Contact the seller to report the issue
  • Verify your address is complete and accurate
