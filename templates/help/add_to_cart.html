Building your order is easy with FarmHub's shopping cart. Learn how to add products and manage quantities before checkout.

Adding Items to Your Cart

Step 1: Browse products and find an item you want to buy.
Step 2: Click on the product to view its details.
Step 3: Select the quantity you want to order using the +/- buttons or by entering a number.
Step 4: Click the "Add to Cart" button.
Step 5: You'll see a confirmation message that the item has been added to your cart.
You can add products from multiple shops to your cart, but orders will be processed separately for each shop.

Viewing Your Cart

Step 1: Click on the cart icon in the navigation bar.
Step 2: You'll see a list of all items in your cart with:
  • Product name and image
  • Price per unit
  • Quantity selected
  • Subtotal for each item
Step 3: At the bottom, you'll see the total amount for your order.

Selecting Items for Checkout

When you have multiple items in your cart:

Step 1: Use the checkboxes next to each item to select which ones you want to purchase.
Step 2: Use "Select All" to choose all items at once.
Step 3: The total will update based on your selections.

Requirements for Adding to Cart

  • Must be logged in: You need a FarmHub account to use the cart
  • Product must be in stock: Out-of-stock items cannot be added
  • Quantity limits: You can only order up to the available stock
Items in your cart are not reserved. Complete your checkout promptly to secure your order, especially for popular items.

Cart Tips

  • Review before checkout: Double-check quantities and prices
  • Consider shipping: Ordering more from one shop may be more efficient
  • Save time: Add multiple items before proceeding to checkout
  • Check availability: Items may become unavailable while in your cart
Your cart is saved to your account, so you can come back later to complete your purchase. However, prices and availability may change.
