Start selling your farm products by listing them on FarmHub. This guide shows you how to create attractive product listings that convert browsers into buyers.

Accessing Product Management

Step 1: Log in to your seller account.
Step 2: From your seller dashboard, click on "Products" in the sidebar.
Step 3: Click the "Add Product" button.

Creating a Product Listing

Step 1: Enter the Product Name
  • Be clear and specific (e.g., "Fresh Lakatan Bananas")
  • Include variety if applicable
Step 2: Upload a Product Image
  • Use a clear, well-lit photo
  • Show the actual product customers will receive
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG
Step 3: Select the Product Category/Type
  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Livestock
  • Poultry
  • Grains
  • Others
Step 4: Set the Price
  • Enter your selling price in Pesos (‚Ç±)
  • Consider your costs and market rates
Step 5: Select the Unit Type
  • Per kilogram (kg)
  • Per piece (pc)
  • Per bundle
  • Per pack
  • Per tray
Step 6: Enter Available Stock
  • How many units do you have available?
  • Update this regularly as stock changes
Step 7: Click "Add Product" or "Save" to create the listing.
Products need to be approved before they appear publicly. Make sure your listings follow FarmHub guidelines.

Pricing Your Products

Consider these factors when setting prices:

  • Production costs: Seeds, labor, equipment
  • Market rates: What are similar products selling for?
  • Quality: Premium products can command higher prices
  • Competition: What are other sellers charging?
  • Delivery costs: Factor in if you're including delivery

Product Approval

After creating a product:

  1. Your product will be submitted for review
  2. Admin reviews the listing for compliance
  3. Once approved, the product becomes visible to buyers
  4. If rejected, you'll be notified with reasons
Approval ensures all products meet quality standards and are accurately represented, building trust with buyers.

Editing Products

To modify an existing product:

  1. Go to your Products page
  2. Find the product you want to edit
  3. Click the "Edit" button
  4. Make your changes
  5. Save the updated listing

Deleting Products

To remove a product:

  1. Go to your Products page
  2. Find the product to delete
  3. Click the "Delete" button
  4. Confirm the deletion
Deleting a product is permanent. Consider setting stock to 0 instead if you might want to sell it again later.
