Need to change your order? FarmHub makes it easy to modify your cart before checkout. Here's how to update quantities or remove items.

Accessing Your Cart

Step 1: Click on the cart icon in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
Step 2: You'll see all items currently in your cart.

Changing Item Quantities

Step 1: Find the item you want to modify in your cart.
Step 2: Look for the quantity selector (usually +/- buttons or a number field).
Step 3: Click "+" to increase the quantity or "-" to decrease it.
Step 4: The subtotal and total will automatically update.
You can only increase quantity up to the available stock. If you need more, check back later or contact the seller.

Removing Items from Cart

Step 1: Find the item you want to remove in your cart.
Step 2: Click the "Remove" button or trash/delete icon next to the item.
Step 3: Confirm the removal if prompted.
Step 4: The item will be removed and your total will update.

Selecting/Deselecting Items

If your cart has checkboxes for selection:

Step 1: Uncheck items you don't want to include in your current order.
Step 2: Those items will remain in your cart but won't be included in checkout.
Step 3: Use "Select All" or "Deselect All" for quick selection.

Emptying Your Entire Cart

To remove all items at once:

Step 1: Look for a "Clear Cart" or "Empty Cart" option.
Step 2: Confirm when prompted (this action cannot be undone).
Step 3: Your cart will be emptied.
Removing items from your cart is permanent. If you want to order them later, you'll need to add them again from the product page.

Common Cart Issues

Item Not Updating?

  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Make sure you're still logged in
  • Check your internet connection

Can't Increase Quantity?

  • You may have reached the maximum available stock
  • The product may have limited stock remaining
  • Contact the seller to ask about more stock

Item Disappeared from Cart?

  • The product may have become unavailable
  • The seller may have removed the listing
  • Check the product page to see if it's still available
It's a good practice to review your cart right before checkout to ensure all items and quantities are correct.
